Vision-Aid’s Digital Accessibility Testing & Training Center (DATTC) offers a comprehensive range of resources for Digital Accessibility Testing.

Vision-Aid offers a professionally structured, state-of-the-art training program at a highly subsidized cost

Our comprehensive and yet highly subsidized training course is designed to help a large number of visually impaired students to acquire deep skills in accessibility testing. Students get to earn Course Completion Certificates from Deque University and other leading Organizations. 

Vision-Aid guides students towards job opportunities in the area of accessibility testing.

Post-Training Placement Support

Beyond Training, Vision-Aid empowers the visually impaired with a range of support services for our visually impaired students and staff

  • Vision-Aid’s support includes coaching, mentoring, resume creation, soft skills training, networking and advocacy. 
  • We work to connect demand and supply, helping to provide a pool of talented testers for the industry at large.
  • Our goal is to see that all our visually impaired students are gainfully employed so they live independently and with dignity, while also making significant positive contributions towards making our digital world a more accessible and inclusive space for all.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to facilitate the creation of an inclusive digital ecosystem for persons with disabilities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer affordable, high-quality digital accessibility testing services for companies and organizations which aspire to create highly accessible digital systems and to offer high quality training programs in digital accessibility to persons with disabilities to help them become competent testers.
